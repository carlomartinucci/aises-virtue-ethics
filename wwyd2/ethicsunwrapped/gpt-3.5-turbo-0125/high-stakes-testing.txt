Establish a Stakeholder Task Force: I would create a Stakeholder Task Force comprising students, parents, teachers, administrators, and community members to collaboratively address the issues surrounding high stakes testing. The Stakeholder Task Force would work towards developing alternative assessment methods that are more holistic and student-centered. This could involve exploring project-based assessments, portfolios, and performance tasks that provide a more comprehensive view of student learning. By involving all stakeholders in the decision-making process, we can create a more inclusive and ethical approach to assessment that prioritizes the well-being and educational experience of students.
