5. Engaging with affected communities, such as elderly and Spanish-speaking customers, demonstrates empathy and a willingness to address the specific needs of vulnerable populations.
6. Collaboration with regulatory authorities and compliance with legal standards is crucial for restoring trust and credibility with external stakeholders.
