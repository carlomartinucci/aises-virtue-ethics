I would inform consumers about the potential risks and privacy concerns associated with home DNA testing kits. This would involve creating educational materials that clearly outline how their genetic information could be used by testing companies, including the possibility of sharing data with third parties, pharmaceutical companies, and law enforcement. Additionally, I would provide guidance on how consumers can protect their privacy, such as being cautious about sharing sensitive information and understanding the implications of consenting to data sharing for research purposes.
